🐾 About Pet Vet AI

AI-powered pet health diagnosis, built for pet owners everywhere.

🤖 What We Do

Pet Vet AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to help you understand your pet's health. Upload a photo, describe symptoms, and get an instant AI-powered diagnosis — anytime, anywhere.

We support dogs, cats, and a growing list of animals. Our symptom checker and worldwide vet finder help you make informed decisions about your pet's care.

💡 Our Mission

We believe every pet deserves quality healthcare guidance, regardless of where you live or what time it is. Pet Vet AI is available 24/7, multilingual, and built to empower pet owners with knowledge.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

Pet Vet AI is an informational tool and is not a substitute for professional veterinary care. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for diagnosis and treatment of your pet's health conditions. In emergencies, contact your nearest emergency vet immediately.

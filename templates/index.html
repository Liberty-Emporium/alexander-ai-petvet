Frequently Asked Questions
Is Pet Vet AI a replacement for a real vet?
No — and we say that clearly on every diagnosis. We help you understand what might be wrong and how urgent it is. For anything serious, you should always see a licensed vet.
Does it really work for farm animals?
Yes. Most pet health apps only support dogs and cats. We were built from the start to support horses, cattle, goats, pigs, chickens, and other farm animals that most apps ignore.
How accurate is the AI?
Very good — but not perfect. The AI is trained on thousands of documented cases. It's a powerful first step, but always follow up with a vet for anything serious.
